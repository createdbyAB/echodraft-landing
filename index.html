01How it works

Three steps. That's it.

Step one

Tap and talk.

Open the app. Start talking. No time limit.

Step two

Answer a question or two.

When you pause, EchoDraft asks a short follow-up. You answer. It listens.

Step three

Read it back.

You get a real draft in the format you picked. Edit, share, or save.
